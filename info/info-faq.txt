Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ #1-
 Q: There's people cheating and joining the other team!  What's going on?
 A: There are admins who have permission to edit OC's.  They are not cheating.
      - These admins cannot earn any hiscores, stats, and they cannot win.

FAQ #2-
 Q: What is speedmode?
 A: speedmode sets your speed to your current flySpeed.  flySpeed can be set
      - by setting cg_flySpeed.  If your client does not have cg_flySpeed, you
      - can also try setting cl_anonymous, handicap, or color1 to non-default
      - non-zero values.  Note that most clients will not let handicap go
      - higher than 100

FAQ #3-
 Q: How do I disable the annoying tesla sound?
 A: Open your console, and type "/set cg_disableWeaponSounds tesla/idle1.wav"

FAQ #4-
 Q: Can I measure my speed or time?
 A: Yes.  Open the console and type "/set cg_printSpeedometer 1" and "/set cg_printTimer 1"
      - (use the same commands with 0 to disable them.  2.0 introduced a nicer method
	  - of measuring with HUD elements.  Type "/set cg_drawSpeedometer 1" and
      - "/set cg_drawPlayerTimer 1" to enable them (notice Player in cg_drawPlayerTimer)

FAQ #5-
 Q: Can I beat an obstacle course without spoiling any solutions?
 A: Yes, type "/hide" in console to hide yourself from non-admin players.  Run the same
     - command again to unhide yourself
